Council

The Feeali Women's Development Council works in partnership with local government and international organizations to create policies and programs that support women's advancement.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive society where women have equal opportunities in education, employment, leadership, and decision-making processes.

Strategic Priorities

  • Economic empowerment through skill development
  • Health and wellness programs
  • Legal aid and advocacy
  • Education and literacy initiatives
  • Political participation and leadership training

Council Structure

The council consists of representatives from various political parties, community leaders, and women's rights advocates who collaborate to develop and implement women-focused policies.
